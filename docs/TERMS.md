## 5. Location Data

Posting on Jawwing requires sharing your GPS coordinates. Here's exactly how we handle location:

- **At post time:** Your GPS coordinates are used to attach your post to a geographic area.
- **Not stored with identity:** Location is stored with the post content, not linked to your IP hash, account, or any persistent user profile.
- **Not tracked over time:** We do not log or retain a history of locations you've posted from. Each post's location is independent.
- **What we store:** A coordinate pair (latitude/longitude) associated with the post. Not a street address. Not a movement history.

By posting, you consent to this use of your location data.
