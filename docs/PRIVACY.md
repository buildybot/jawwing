# Jawwing Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 10, 2026  
**Effective:** March 10, 2026

Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data Jawwing collects, what it doesn't, and how we use it. We've kept it as plain as possible.

---

## 1. The Short Version

- We collect almost nothing
- Your email address is hashed — we never see or store the real thing
- We use your location to place posts, but don't track you
- We don't sell your data. Ever.
- Posts expire after 24 hours and are deleted
- You can delete your account and all data anytime

---

## 2. What We Collect

### What We DO Collect

| Data | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Hashed email address** | Verify you're a real person; prevent duplicate accounts |
| **Location (at post time)** | Attach your post to a place |
| **Device info** | Basic technical data (OS, browser/app version, IP address) for security and abuse prevention |
| **Post content** | To display it on the platform |
| **Votes (up/down)** | To rank content |
| **Account handle** | Auto-generated; used to identify your session |

Location is collected **only at the moment you post** — we don't track your location over time or store it persistently in your profile.

### What We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Your real name
- ❌ Your actual email address (only the hash)
- ❌ Browsing history outside Jawwing
- ❌ Contacts or address book
- ❌ Photos or media from your device (unless you explicitly attach them to a post)
- ❌ Precise GPS history or movement patterns
- ❌ Payment information (Jawwing is free)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

- **Run the platform** — display posts, apply votes, serve location-based feeds
- **Verify accounts** — confirm email ownership to prevent spam and bots
- **Moderate content** — our AI moderation system reviews posts against our Mod Constitution
- **Prevent abuse** — detect and block spammers, bad actors, and policy violations
- **Improve Jawwing** — aggregate, anonymized analytics to understand how the platform is used (not linked to you personally)

We do **not** use your data for:
- Targeted advertising
- Selling to data brokers
- Building behavioral profiles
- Training external AI models on identifiable user data

---

## 4. Who We Share Data With

**Nobody, with narrow exceptions.**

| Recipient | Circumstances |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Resend** | Email verification only — they deliver the verification code to your email address. Resend's privacy policy applies during that step. |
| **Turso** (database) | Our US-based database provider stores your data. They're a processor, not a controller — they don't use your data for their own purposes. |
| **Vercel** (hosting) | Our infrastructure provider. Same deal — processor only. |
| **Law enforcement** | Only if legally required by a valid court order, subpoena, or warrant. We'll notify you if legally permitted to do so. |

We don't sell data. We don't share it with advertisers. We don't do data broker deals.

---

## 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| Posts | **24 hours** — then automatically deleted |
| Location data (from posts) | Deleted with the post (24 hours) |
| Account data (handle, hashed email) | Until you delete your account |
| Device/session logs | Up to 90 days for security purposes |
| Moderation logs | Up to 1 year (abuse prevention) |

When you delete your account, we delete your account data within **30 days**. Some residual data (e.g., anonymized moderation logs) may be retained longer for safety purposes.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you can:

- **Delete your account** — via settings, anytime. We'll wipe your data within 30 days.
- **Request your data** — email privacy@jawwing.com and we'll send you what we have.
- **Correct your data** — there's not much to correct (no name, no email), but reach out if something's wrong.
- **Object to processing** — if you think we're processing your data unlawfully, contact us.

We'll respond to requests within **30 days**.

### California Residents (CCPA)
You have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of its sale (we don't sell it). To exercise these rights, email **privacy@jawwing.com**. We won't discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

### EU/UK Residents (GDPR)
Our lawful bases for processing are **legitimate interest** (security, abuse prevention) and **contract performance** (running the service you signed up for). You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your data, and to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority. Contact **privacy@jawwing.com** for requests.

### Children (COPPA)
Jawwing is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe we have, contact us at **legal@jawwing.com** and we'll delete it immediately.

---

## 7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

- Email addresses are **hashed using a one-way function** — we can't reverse them
- Data is stored on Turso (US-based, encrypted at rest)
- HTTPS everywhere
- Access controls on our internal systems

No system is 100% secure. If there's a breach that affects your data, we'll notify you as required by law.

---

## 8. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies — only what's needed to keep you logged in and prevent CSRF attacks. We don't use tracking pixels, ad cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

If you use the web app, your browser may store a session token. Clear it anytime by logging out.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy materially, we'll post a notice on the platform. The "Last updated" date at the top always reflects the current version. Continued use after the notice period = acceptance.
